Understanding the Problem

Key Healthcare Challenges We Solve with AI

Healthcare organisations face mounting pressure: rising costs, staff burnout, diagnostic bottlenecks, and complex regulatory requirements. AI addresses each at scale.

📝

Clinical Documentation Burden

Manual clinical documentation consumes 35–40% of physician time, directly contributing to burnout and reducing time available for patient care.

🔬

Diagnostic Imaging Backlogs

High imaging volumes create queues that delay diagnosis of critical conditions, particularly in radiology, pathology, and dermatology workflows.

🔗

Fragmented Patient Data

Patient information locked in siloed EHR systems prevents holistic clinical views and blocks population-level analytics that could improve outcomes.

⚖️

Regulatory Complexity

HIPAA compliance, FDA AI/ML SaMD guidelines, and evolving state regulations create barriers that slow AI adoption and increase compliance costs.

What We Build

Our AI Solutions for Healthcare

From ambient clinical documentation to advanced imaging AI, our healthcare solutions are designed for real clinical environments and validated on real patient data.

🎙️

Clinical Note Generation

AI-powered ambient documentation captures physician speech and generates structured clinical notes, SOAP notes, and discharge summaries — cutting documentation time by up to 65%.

🏥

Medical Image Analysis

Computer vision models for radiology, pathology, and dermatology that detect anomalies, prioritise urgent cases, and provide quantitative measurements from CT, MRI, X-ray, and whole-slide imaging.

📊

EHR Data Integration & NLP

Clinical NLP pipelines that extract structured information from unstructured EHR data via FHIR APIs — enabling longitudinal patient views and population health analytics across fragmented systems.

📈

Patient Risk Stratification

ML models that identify high-risk patients — for readmission, disease progression, or complications — months before traditional warning signs, enabling proactive interventions that reduce ER visits.

💊

Drug Interaction & Clinical Decision Support

AI-powered CDS tools that surface relevant clinical guidelines, flag drug interactions, and recommend evidence-based pathways at the point of care — integrated directly into the EHR workflow.

💬

Patient Engagement Chatbots

HIPAA-compliant conversational AI for appointment scheduling, symptom triage, medication reminders, post-discharge follow-up, and patient education — reducing administrative call volume by 40%.

Proven Results

Real-World AI Use Cases in Healthcare

These represent the types of outcomes our healthcare AI systems deliver across hospital networks, health insurers, and radiology providers.

Hospital Network

AI Clinical Note Generator

Problem: Physicians spent 3+ hours per day on EHR documentation after patient visits, contributing to burnout.

Solution: Ambient AI documentation system using speech recognition and clinical NLP to auto-generate structured notes, integrated with Epic EHR.

Radiology Network

Critical Finding Triage AI

Problem: High scan volumes created 72-hour read queues, delaying diagnosis of critical conditions including pulmonary emboli and intracranial haemorrhage.

Solution: Computer vision model trained on 2M+ annotated scans to flag and prioritise critical findings within seconds of acquisition.

40% faster critical finding identification vs. standard radiologist queue
Health Insurance

Patient Risk Stratification

Problem: High-risk members were not identified until expensive ER visits or hospitalisations had already occurred.

Solution: ML risk stratification model analysing claims data, lab results, and pharmacy records to identify high-risk members 6 months before typical clinical warning signs.

28% reduction in ER visits through proactive care management
