RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) connects a language model to your own knowledge base — documents, databases, or live data — so the model answers using your specific information rather than only its training data. Use RAG when you need an AI assistant that is accurate about your company's proprietary content, avoids hallucinations, and can be updated in real time without retraining the model.

Process

Timeline depends on scope and complexity. A focused Proof of Concept (PoC) typically takes 2–4 weeks. An MVP with core AI features usually requires 6–12 weeks. Enterprise-grade production systems are delivered in phases starting with a 2-week discovery and architecture sprint, followed by iterative build cycles. We provide a detailed timeline in your project proposal.

We follow a structured, transparent process: (1) Discovery Call — align on goals, constraints, and success metrics. (2) Architecture & Proposal — technical design, stack selection, timeline, and pricing. (3) Iterative Build — two-week sprints with demos and feedback cycles. (4) Testing & Evaluation — model benchmarking, integration testing, and security review. (5) Deployment — to your cloud or on-premises infrastructure. (6) Handover & Support — documentation, team training, and ongoing monitoring.

Security & Compliance

Data privacy is built into our engineering process from day one. We implement end-to-end encryption, role-based access controls, audit logging, and data minimisation principles. For sensitive workloads, we deploy models within your own private infrastructure so no training data or inference requests ever leave your environment.

We are experienced with HIPAA, GDPR, and PCI-DSS compliance requirements and design AI systems to meet these standards. We can sign Business Associate Agreements (BAAs) for HIPAA-covered clients and implement all required technical safeguards. Note: compliance ultimately also depends on your organisation's broader data governance programme.

You do. Upon full payment, all intellectual property — including source code, trained model weights, datasets, and documentation — transfers to you. This is stated explicitly in our standard contract. We retain no rights to use your project's IP for any other purpose.

Our AI security practices include: input validation and sanitisation, system prompt hardening, output filtering for sensitive data leakage, rate limiting, monitoring for anomalous patterns, and red-team adversarial testing of LLM-powered applications before production launch.
