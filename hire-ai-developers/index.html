How It Works

5-Step Hiring Process

From requirements to first sprint in 48–72 hours. Our streamlined process eliminates the typical weeks-long recruitment cycle.

  1. Share your requirements

    Complete a 5-minute requirements form or book a 30-minute discovery call with our team.

  2. Candidate matching within 24 hours

    We present 2–3 pre-vetted profiles matched to your technical requirements and time zone.

  3. Technical interview & profile review

    Interview shortlisted candidates and review their portfolios, test results, and reference summaries.

  4. Agree on engagement model and contract

    Choose your engagement model, finalise the contract, and confirm IP assignment and NDA terms.

  5. Onboard and start sprinting within 48–72 hours

    Your engineer joins your tools, attends standups, and begins delivering value from day one.
